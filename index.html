About me

I'm Adarsh Remesh, an architecture graduate from the School of Planning and Architecture, New Delhi. My design approach is grounded in empathy, rooted in context, and shaped by the cultures and climates I design for. Whether working on a marine life park along Kerala's fragile coast, developing tribal knowledge hubs in remote villages, or contributing to large-scale institutional projects at Studio Lotus, I've always been drawn to architecture that resonates with people and place. My practice combines systems thinking, hands-on building logic, and a love for storytelling—across drawing, code, and craft. I'm currently seeking opportunities to collaborate with studios where design is both poetic and purposeful.
